Admission Process

1

Inquiry

Fill out the inquiry form or contact us to learn more about our programs and schedule a campus tour.

2

Campus Tour

Visit our facilities, meet our staff, and see our classrooms in action. This helps you understand our environment.

3

Application

Complete the formal application form with details about your child and family. Provide necessary documentation.

4

Assessment

Your child participates in a gentle assessment to determine the best program placement based on developmental level.

5

Interview

Meet with our admission staff to discuss your child's needs, expectations, and answer any questions you may have.

6

Enrollment

Upon acceptance, complete enrollment paperwork and discuss fee structure. Your child starts with an orientation visit.

Admission Requirements

Documentation

Birth certificate, immunization records, passport/ID, health assessment form, and proof of address.

Health Screening

Up-to-date vaccinations as per local requirements, medical check-up, and allergy/dietary information.

Developmental Level

Child's development should be appropriate for the age group. We provide recommendations for optimal placement.

Enrollment Agreement

Parents sign the enrollment agreement outlining school policies, fees, and payment terms.

Contact Information

Emergency contacts, phone numbers, and parent communication preferences for regular updates.

Personal Details

Family background, dietary needs, behavioral information, and any special requirements.
