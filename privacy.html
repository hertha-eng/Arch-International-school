Privacy Policy

How ARCH International School handles information shared through this website.

Important Information

Your privacy matters to us

This page explains how we handle information submitted through our website, including contact messages, admissions inquiries, and donation-related communication.

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Information We Collect

We may collect information you choose to provide, such as your name, email address, phone number, family details shared in inquiries, and any message you send through our forms or contact channels.

How We Use Information

We use the information you share to respond to inquiries, process admissions-related communication, coordinate school visits, provide updates you request, and improve how we support families and visitors.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared only when reasonably necessary to operate school services, process communications, comply with legal obligations, or protect the safety of our school community.

Third-Party Services

If you use external forms, maps, or payment-related services, those services may collect information under their own privacy policies. Please review those policies where applicable.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information shared with us. However, no internet-based system can be guaranteed completely secure.

Your Choices

You may contact us to ask about information you have shared, request corrections, or ask us to remove information where appropriate and legally permitted.

Contact About Privacy

For privacy-related questions, contact us at archinternationalsc@gmail.com or via WhatsApp at +256 701 211 117.
