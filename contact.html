Frequently Asked Questions

What are your admission requirements?

We accept children from ages 18 months to 4 years old. There are no specific academic requirements for admission. We assess each child’s developmental level to support the right program placement.

What are your operating hours?

We operate from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Extended care options are available for working parents. We are closed on weekends and major holidays.

Do you provide meals and snacks?

Yes, we provide nutritious, age-appropriate meals and snacks throughout the day. We also accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies when families share those needs with us.

What is your teacher-to-student ratio?

We maintain low teacher-to-student ratios: nursery 1:4, pre-school 1:6, and kindergarten 1:8. This helps every child receive individual attention and support.

Can I visit the school before enrolling?

Yes. We encourage parents and guardians to book a school visit. You will have the opportunity to meet our staff and see our learning environment in person.

What is your sick child policy?

If a child has fever, is vomiting, or shows signs of contagious illness, they should stay home. We require a 24-hour illness-free period before returning to school.
