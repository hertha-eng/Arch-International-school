About ARCH International School

Discover our story, our purpose, and the values that shape every child’s experience.

Who We Are

A nurturing early years school built on curiosity, creativity, and care

Learning Beyond Borders

Founded on the principles of curiosity, creativity, and compassion, ARCH International School exists to inspire young minds and growing hearts. We believe early childhood education provides the foundation for confidence, character, and lifelong learning.

Our school community is dedicated to providing a safe, engaging, and inclusive learning environment where every child is known, encouraged, and supported. Through caring relationships and thoughtful teaching, we help children develop academically, socially, emotionally, and physically.

Our Foundation

Mission, vision, and values that guide our school

Our Mission

To provide a holistic, play-based learning experience that nurtures the intellectual, emotional, social, and physical development of every child in our care.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution recognized for excellence in early childhood education, where children develop into confident, curious, and compassionate individuals.

Our Values

Respect, integrity, excellence, inclusivity, and community. We value each child’s uniqueness and strive to bring out the best in every learner.

Why Families Choose Us

A school community designed to help children thrive

Families trust ARCH because we combine strong care, purposeful learning, and a welcoming environment where children can grow with confidence.

Experienced Teachers

Our team includes committed early childhood educators with professional experience and a genuine passion for guiding young learners.

Play-Based Learning

We believe children learn best through play, exploration, and discovery. Our curriculum encourages active participation and creativity.

Safe Environment

Safety and security remain central to our daily operations, helping children learn in spaces that feel calm, secure, and welcoming.

Community Focus

We value strong relationships with parents and guardians because meaningful partnership helps each child feel supported at home and at school.

Quality Programs

From nursery through kindergarten, our programs are thoughtfully shaped to support age-appropriate growth, confidence, and readiness.

Global Perspective

We introduce children to diverse cultures, ideas, and perspectives, helping them grow with openness, respect, and curiosity.
